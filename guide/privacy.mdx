# Privacy and Storage

PokeSVG is local-first:

- No accounts
- No server-side storage
- No external APIs

## What is stored

Your dex and settings are stored in your browser's `localStorage`:

- Dex collection: `pokesvg.collection.v2`
- Settings: `pokesvg.settings.v1`

## Clearing data

Clearing site data for the PokeSVG origin will remove your dex and settings.
